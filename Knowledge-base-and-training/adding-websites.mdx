---
title: "Adding Websites"
---

Connect a website, blog, or public pages so the AI Double can answer questions using site content. The AI extracts visible text from the pages added.

## **How to add websites**

1. Open **Knowledge** (find it in the sidebar under **Configure** Or on your Double in homepage)
2. Click **Website URL** in Data Sources section
3. Choose **Single Page** or **Multiple Pages**
4. Paste the website URL
5. Click **Crawl website and train**

Websites appear in the **Links** section on the same page.

## **What to know**

- **Single Page** - Adds one specific page
- **Multiple Pages** - Crawls entire site or multiple pages
- **Only visible text is imported** - Images and hidden content aren't captured
- **Previously crawled pages are excluded** - Prevents duplicate content
- **Paid articles not supported** - Content behind paywalls won't work
- **Social profiles not supported** - Use social media connectors instead

## **Best practices**

Add websites that contain:

- Services or product pages
- About page and team information
- Blog posts or articles
- Support documentation
- Case studies or testimonials

Use a **Single Page** for specific content. Use **Multiple Pages** to import the entire site at once.

## **Managing websites**

To delete a website, click the trash icon next to the URL in the **Links** section.
