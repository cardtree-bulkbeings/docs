---
title: "What if my sources have conflicting information?"
---

When multiple sources contain different information about the same topic, your AI Double prioritizes sources in this order:

1. **Context Instructions and custom text** - Most specific and direct
2. **Recently uploaded PDFs** - Newer files take priority
3. **Website content** - Your site pages
4. **Social media** - General background context

## **How to fix conflicts**

If your AI gives inconsistent answers:

- Check which sources might conflict
- Update or delete outdated sources
- Use Context Instructions to clarify which information is correct
- Upload newer versions of PDFs to override old content

Your AI Double tries to use the most relevant and recent information available.
