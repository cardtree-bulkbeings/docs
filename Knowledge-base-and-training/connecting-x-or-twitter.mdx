Import tweets from an X/Twitter profile to train the AI Double on social media content and perspectives.

## **How to connect X/Twitter**

1. Open **Knowledge** (find it in the sidebar under **Configure** Or on your Double in homepage)
2. Click **X / Twitter** in the Data Sources section
3. Enter Twitter/X username or profile URL
4. Select content type: **Tweets**, **Replies**, or **All**
5. Click **Crawl tweets and train**

X/Twitter content appears in the **Social's Data** section on the same page.

## **What to know**

- **Only public profiles work** - Private accounts can't be accessed
- **Recent tweets are imported** - Latest posts from the profile
- **Engagement data included** - Likes, retweets, and replies are captured
- **One profile at a time** - Process one account per session

## **Content types**

- **Tweets** - Original posts only
- **Replies** - Responses to others
- **All** - Everything from the profile

## **Best practices**

Connect X/Twitter to:

- Share expertise or insights on Twitter
- Reference tweets in AI responses
- Include industry commentary or opinions
- Build personal brand on X

## **Managing X/Twitter connection**

To disconnect, click the trash icon next to the profile in the **Social's Data** section.
