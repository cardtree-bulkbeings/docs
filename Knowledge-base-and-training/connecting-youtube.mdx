---
title: "Connecting YouTube"
---

Import YouTube videos to train the AI Double on video content. Videos are automatically transcribed so the AI can reference what has been shared.

## **How to connect YouTube**

1. Open **Knowledge** (find it in the sidebar under **Configure**)
2. Click **YouTube** in the Data Sources section
3. Paste YouTube video URLs (one or multiple)
4. Click **Transcribe and train**

YouTube content appears in the **Social's Data** section on the same page.

## **What to know**

- Videos are transcribed using available captions
- Only videos with captions can be processed
- Video titles, descriptions, and timestamps are included
- Processing time varies by video length

## **Best practices**

Connect YouTube to:

- Create educational or tutorial content
- Share expertise through video
- Reference explanations in AI responses
- Include podcasts or interviews on YouTube

## **Managing YouTube connection**

To disconnect, click the trash icon next to the video in the **Social's Data** section.
