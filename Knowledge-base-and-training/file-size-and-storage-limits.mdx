---
title: "What are the file size and storage limits?"
---

File size and storage capacity depend on your Double AI plan.

## **Check your limits**

1. Go to **Knowledge Base**
2. Your current storage usage appears at the top
3. [<u>View pricing</u>](https://thedouble.ai/pricing) for plan details

## **What counts toward storage**

- **PDFs** - Count toward your storage limit
- **Websites** - Don't count toward storage
- **Social media** - Don't count toward storage
- **Custom text** - Minimal storage impact

If you reach your limit, delete old files or upgrade your plan.
