---
title: "Presentation Flow"
---

**Visitor initiates:**

1. Visitor asks about your product or service
2. Your Double offers: "Would you like me to walk you through the features?"
3. Visitor accepts

**Presentation starts:**

1. Double speaks the opening message
2. Slide 1 appears on screen
3. Double (optionally) introduces the slide
4. Visitor can read, listen, or both

**During presentation:**

- Visitor clicks Next or Previous to navigate
- Double does NOT speak during slide transitions (visitor controls pacing)
- Each slide remains visible until visitor navigates
- Visitor can ask questions anytime (pauses presentation)

**Presentation ends:**

1. Visitor reaches final slide or clicks End
2. Double speaks closing message
3. Visitor returns to normal chat
