### **Agent Page vs Card Page**

Every Double comes with two public pages — an Agent page and a Card page. They serve different purposes, but work together to give visitors a complete picture of who you are.

### **Agent Page**

Your Agent page is where visitors come to have a live conversation with your AI Double — via text or voice.

- URL format: thedouble.ai/\[handle\]
- Example: thedouble.ai/thunder

This is your AI-first presence. Visitors land here to ask questions, get answers, and interact with your Double in real time. The agent page is designed to keep the focus on the conversation.

### **Card Page**

Your Card page is your smart digital profile — a visual overview of who you are, what you do, and how to connect with you.

- URL format: thedouble.ai/\[handle\]/card
- Example: thedouble.ai/thunder/card

This is your profile-first presence. Visitors land here to browse your work experience, skills, links, contact details, and more — at their own pace. Think of it as a smart business card that lives on the web.

#### **What's the difference?**

|  | **Agent Page** | **Card Page** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Primary purpose** | Live AI conversation | Browse your profile |
| **Main interaction** | Chat or voice with your Double | Scroll through your identity card |
| **Best for** | Visitors who want to talk | Visitors who want to explore |
| **URL** | thedouble.ai/handle | thedouble.ai/handle/card |

#### **Which one should I share?**

It depends on what you want visitors to do first.

- Share your **Agent page** when you want visitors to start a conversation — great for networking, cold outreach, and inbound inquiries.
- Share your **Card page** when you want visitors to browse your profile first — great for job applications, portfolio sharing, and introductions.

**Note:** Both pages are always live. Visitors can switch between them — the Agent page has a **Card** button and the Card page links back to your Double.
