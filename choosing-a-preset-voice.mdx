---
title: "Choosing a Preset Voice"
---

Select a preset voice if you prefer not to clone your own. Light and high quality voice presets are available

**How to choose a preset voice**

1. Go to Voice (find it in the sidebar or in your Double AI Twin)
2. Browse the available preset voices
3. Click any preset to preview it
4. Select the preset to apply it

**What to know**

- Presets use the light voice model - Faster and more resource-efficient than cloned HQ voices
- Accent and gender options available - Choose from the available options in the preset list
- Preset can be replaced anytime - Upload a clone later to switch to HQ voice
