## **Cloning Your Voice** 

Clone a real voice so the AI Double sounds like the user. A short, clean audio sample is all that is needed. 

#### **How to clone your voice**

1. Go to Voice (find it in the sidebar or in your Double AI Twin) 

2. Click Add New Clone

3. Enter a name for the clone (e.g. "My Voice") 

4. Add an optional description 

5. Choose to Upload a file or Record directly into the microphone

6. Submit to create the clone The cloned voice appears in the Voice section and is used automatically for voice conversations.

#### **Recording tips **

● Record in a quiet, noise-free environment

● Use a good-quality microphone or headset

● On mobile: use Voice Memo (iOS) or Dolby On (Android) for better noise cancellation

● Read the sample phrase shown on screen - it is designed to capture the full range of sounds and emotion What to know 

● Minimum 15 seconds, maximum 30 seconds - Longer samples do not improve quality beyond 30 seconds 

● Maximum file size: 5 MB 

● Quality of clone matches quality of recording - A clear, close-mic recording gives the best result 

● One active clone at a time - Create a new clone to replace the existing one
