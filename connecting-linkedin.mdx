---
title: "Connecting Linkedin"
---

Import posts from a LinkedIn profile to train the AI Double on professional content and insights.

## **How to connect LinkedIn**

1. Open **Knowledge** (find it in the sidebar under **Configure** Or on your Double in homepage)
2. Click **LinkedIn** in the Data Sources section
3. Paste LinkedIn profile URL
4. Click **Crawl posts and train**

LinkedIn content appears in the **Social's Data** section on the same page.

## **What to know**

- Only public profiles and posts are accessible
- Recent posts are imported from the profile
- Post attachments and links are preserved

## **Best practices**

Connect LinkedIn to:

- Share professional insights or expertise
- Post industry commentary or analysis
- Reference thought leadership in AI responses
- Build personal brand on LinkedIn

## **Managing LinkedIn connection**

To disconnect, click the trash icon next to the profile in the **Social's Data** section.
