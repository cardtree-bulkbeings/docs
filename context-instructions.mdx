---
title: "Context Instructions"
---

Provide background details about work, expertise, and focus areas so the AI Double has full context on who someone is and what they do.

## **How to add Context Instructions**

1. Open **Knowledge** (find it in the sidebar under **Configure**)
2. Click **Context Instructions** at the bottom of the Data Sources section
3. Type context in the text field (up to 1,500 characters)
4. Click **Save Instructions**

Context Instructions apply across all knowledge sources.

## **What to know**

- **1,500 character limit** - Keep it focused and clear
- **Applies to all sources** - Works alongside PDFs, websites, and social content
- **Can be updated anytime** - Update context as needed

## **Best practices**

Use Context Instructions to add:

- Specialization or niche focus
- Industry or market
- Key background not in other sources
- Important context about approach
- Details that help qualify conversations

**Example:** "I'm a corporate lawyer specializing in intellectual property. Focus on legal precedents and case law when providing context."

## **Managing Context Instructions**

To edit, click **Context Instructions** again and update the text. Changes save automatically when clicking **Save Instructions**.
