---
title: "Managing Knowledge Sources"
---

Edit, update, or remove knowledge sources to keep the AI Double's information accurate and current.

## **Viewing sources**

All knowledge sources are organized in the Knowledge Base section:

- **Social's Data** - LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and YouTube connections
- **Files** - Uploaded PDFs
- **Images** - Add charts, diagrams, and visual content
- **Links** - Connected websites and pages
- **Text** - Custom text entries
- **LLM Knowledge** - Add a background context to your AI

## **How to update sources**

- **Custom text:** Click the edit icon, update content, and save
- **Context Instructions:** Click Context Instructions, make changes, and save
- **PDFs:** Delete and upload the new version
- **Websites:** Delete and add the updated URL
- **LLM Knowledge** - Delete and add new instructions
- **Images** - Delete and upload new images
- **Social media (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube):** Delete and reconnect to refresh content

## **How to delete sources**

Click the trash icon next to any source to remove it. The AI Double stops using that information immediately.

## **Best practices**

- Review sources regularly to keep information current
- Remove outdated PDFs or websites
- Update custom text as services change
- Reconnect social media periodically for fresh content
