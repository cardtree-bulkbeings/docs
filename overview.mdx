The Knowledge Base is your AI Double's brain. Add files, websites, and social content to fill it with information about you. The more knowledge you provide, the better your AI represents your expertise - across profile chats, website chatbots, and voice conversations.

## **Why Knowledge Base matters**

A strong Knowledge Base makes your AI Double more effective:

- Answers questions about your expertise
- Explains your services clearly
- Qualifies leads accurately
- Performs consistently across chat and voice

Better knowledge = better conversations.

## **What you can add**

Train your AI Double with multiple source types:

- **PDFs** - Documents, portfolios, case studies
- **Custom text** - Information you write directly
- **Websites** - Your site, blog, or public pages
- **Images** - Add charts, diagrams, and visual content
- **LLM Knowledge** - Add a background context to your AI
- **Substack** - Posts and articles from a publication
- **LinkedIn** **(Paid)** - Professional posts and insights
- **X/Twitter (Soon)** - Tweets and threads
- **YouTube (Paid)** - Video transcripts

Your AI Double combines all sources for complete answers.
