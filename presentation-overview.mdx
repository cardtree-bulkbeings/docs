title: "Presentation Overview"
---

Present slide decks directly to visitors through chat. Your Double explains each slide using AI instructions and your voice.

**What Presentations do**

- Uploads slide decks (PowerPoint, Google Slides, PDFs)
- Presents slides one by one based on AI instructions you provide
- Speaks using your cloned voice
- Embeds on your website or shares as a standalone link

**Best for**

- Product demos and pitches
- Sales presentations and case studies
- Training or educational walkthroughs
- Webinars and live presentations
