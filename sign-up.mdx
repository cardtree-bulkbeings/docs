Create your account with Double AI

### There are 3 ways of creating an account with Double AI

\
1. Using Google Account\
2. Using Linkedin Account\
3. Using Email & Password

**1. Using Google Account**\
On the Signup page Click on “Continue with Google” → Select your preferred Google Account → Choose your Cardname

**2. Using Linkedin**\
Click on “Continue with Linkedin” → Sign-in to Linkedin → Choose your Cardname

**3. With Email**\
Enter Your Details and set password → Verify your Email → Login →  Choose your Cardname
