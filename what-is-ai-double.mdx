---
title: "What is AI Double"
---

AI Double is a digital twin powered by AI that represents you or your business. It communicates in your voice, knows your expertise, and engages with visitors through text and voice conversations - 24/7.

## **How AI Double works**

AI Double combines three key elements:

### **1. Knowledge Base & Training Words**

- Learns from your PDFs, websites, social media, and custom text
- Understands your work, services, and expertise
- References your content in conversations

### **2. Personality**

- Matches your communication style and tone
- Controls creativity, confidence, and energy
- Speaks as you (first person) or about you (third person)

### **3. Behavior**

- Follows custom instructions you set
- Qualifies leads and guides conversations
- Handles off-topic questions appropriately

## **What AI Double can do**

- **Answer questions** - About your expertise, services, and background
- **Qualify leads** - Capture contact information and route conversations
- **Engage visitors** - Through chat on your profile or embedded on your website
- **Voice conversations** - Speak with visitors using your cloned voice
- **Stay consistent** - Represents you accurately across all touchpoints

## **Where AI Double appears**

- **Profile chats** - Visitors can chat directly on your Double profile
- **Website chatbots** - Embed on your website as a chat bubble, inline widget, or modern bar
- **Voice calls** - Visitors can have voice conversations with your AI Double

## **For individuals and teams**

**Individuals** get their own AI Double with personal knowledge and voice.

**Teams** share knowledge across the organization while each member has their own personalized AI Double. Knowledge flows from Company Brain → Team Knowledge → Individual Knowledge.
